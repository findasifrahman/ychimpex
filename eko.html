Solar Measurement and Environmental Solutions

Founded in 1927 in Osaka, Japan, EKO Instruments has been at the forefront of environmental and scientific measurement for nearly a century. 
As one of Japan’s first meteorological instrument manufacturers, EKO has consistently set industry benchmarks with groundbreaking innovations 
that empower researchers, scientists, and professionals worldwide to understand better and protect our planet.
