About Securiton

Company

Securiton AG

We belong to the Swiss Securitas Group and have been entirely at the service of technical security since our founding in 1948. With our pioneering spirit and far-sighted approach we have since developed into an acknowledged specialist for high-standard security. Many years of partnership-based co-operation with customers, consultants, official bodies and authorities are proof of our confidence in comprehensive know-how and quality. We offer totally reliable fire detector systems for any kind of environment – from hotel rooms to industrial warehouses and road tunnels. Leading-edge technology, superior know-how and our in-depth experience all combine to prevent damage by fire.

Learn more

Company

Swiss Securitas Group

The Securitas Group is the market leader in Switzerland for security services and technical security equipment. More than 16,000 employees in various companies are at the service of the Securitas Group in Switzerland, Europe and beyond. Together we protect people, safeguard property and ensure quality of life.
